Delivery Options

During the checkout process, you can select your preferred delivery option and we will provide an estimated delivery date based on your selection. We offer the following delivery options:

1. Same Day Delivery
  1. We offer free same day delivery for orders over 49,99€ within Berlin (maximum distance from our Shop: 25km).
  2. Smaller orders will incur a delivery-charge based onyour distance (1€ per km / minimum order value: 20€).
  3. The Orders will be deliveredby us.
  4. We guarantee same day delivery for every order until 18:00.
  5. Orders after 18:00 will be delivered the next working day (Monday - Saturday).
2. Standard Delivery (DHL/DPD)
  1. Free for all orders above 49,99€.
  2. Smaller orders will incur a shipping charge of 4,99€.
  3. The package is expected to be delivered the next working day, but it can take up to three days .
3. Click and Collect
  1. You have the option to collect your groceries directly from our Shop. Store Opening Times: Monday to Saturday from 10:00 to 20:00.
